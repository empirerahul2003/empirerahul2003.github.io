About Me

Hello! I'm Rahul Kumar, a Master's student in Physics at the Central University of Himachal Pradesh. I'm deeply fascinated by quantum computing, data science, and exploring how mathematical concepts like Geometric Algebra can revolutionize our understanding of the physical world.

From a young age, I was captivated by the mysteries of the universe, which led me to pursue a career in physics. During my studies, I developed a keen interest in the intersections of physics and computer science, particularly in quantum computing and data science, which I continue to explore through research and projects.

My core interests lie in quantum computing and mathematical frameworks that simplify complex concepts. I enjoy working on projects that involve advanced mathematical techniques, and I'm always eager to learn more about the applications of AI in physics. Skills I bring include Python, data analysis, and a deep understanding of mathematical modelling in physics.

Currently, I'm working on a project that applies the Phase Function Method to nucleon-nucleon scattering, aiming to deepen our understanding of nuclear forces. Additionally, I'm developing a web app for chatbots, which allows users to select and deploy custom templates.

Outside of academics, I'm an avid reader and enjoy coding small projects for fun. I believe in lifelong learning and often take time to explore new areas, whether it's in physics, technology, or even philosophy.

If you're interested in connecting, feel free to reach out! I'm always excited to collaborate, discuss ideas, and explore new opportunities.
